Case Study #2

Dallas Crime

What I Did:a few other students and I were given the information from the Dallas Police Department for 2014-2015. We were tasked with using various data mining strategies to create a report in the style of one that could be given to the Dallas Police. I used various techniques, such as classification, association, and clustering to find trends and patterns in the data.

With these insights we presented the report with a full business understanding of the implications and time that would be required to change the current protocol. It was important to us that we know the deeper kernels of insight in our data, and present them with the best way to use them, and how to support their validity with user/field research.
