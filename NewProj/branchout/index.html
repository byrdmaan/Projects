What is Branchout?


Branchout is an application designed to get you more involved in your community. Whether you are new to the area or don't know what to do on a friday night, use Branchout to see events happening near you. We allow users to create new events and pull in updated event information from major sites for you so that you can view everything in one place. Enjoy!
