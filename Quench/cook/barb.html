Slow-Cooked Barbacoa

Ingredients

5 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
5 chipotle chiles in adobo
0.5 teaspoons cinnamon
4 pounds Chuck Roast
4 anaheim chiles
7 garlic cloves
3 tablespoons brown sugar
4 teaspoons dried oregano
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 sweet onion
3 bay leaves
s + p to taste

Crucify Your Mind

Rodriguez

perdido en un mundo de confusión:
I am Joaquín, lost in a world of confusion,
caught up in the whirl of a gringo society,

  1. Soak chiles in boiling water until tender. Carefully remove the stems and seeds. Reserve the chile water.

  2. Place the chiles, chipotle chiles, onion, garlic, vinegar, and brown sugar in the blender. Pour the chile water over the top. Puree until smooth.

  3. Place the chuck roast in a large slow cooker. Add the bay leaves, cumin, oregano, and cinnamon. Pour the chile puree over the top. Cover the slow cooker and turn on low for 8-12 hours.

  4. Shred beef with fork into the chile sauce. Cover and keep warm until ready to eat.

  5. Garnish with diced onions and cilantro and serve with a lime wedge.
