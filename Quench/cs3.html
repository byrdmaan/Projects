Branchout

Branchout was a semester long project that was part of a joint class of databases and GUI to make a fully database and API supported app, that is meant to rely on Human-Centered Design principles. My team of 7 worked on Branchout, a community-centric event app. We supported our idea by doing interviews with other people, generally college students or urban professionals, and by creating personas from the stories we found in those interviews. Most people were using Facebook or word of mouth to find local events, and they wanted a way to find local events, and recommendations personalized for them.

Branchout was a web and android application that allowed users to search for events, create them, save them for later, and export to their calendar. Our decisions were always linked to our personas that we created, and had a design first method of feature creation.
